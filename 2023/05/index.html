05/30/2023    Potential sanctions for citing fake cases made up by ChatGPT:

              https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2023/05/lawyer-cited-6-fake-cases-made-up-by-chatgpt-judge-calls-it-unprecedented/

              At least one judge (ND Tex.) is now requiring an certificate
              that no part of any filing was written by a generative AI:

              https://www.txnd.uscourts.gov/judge/judge-brantley-starr

              "All attorneys appearing before the Court must file on the
              docket a certificate attesting either that no portion of
              the filing was drafted by generative artificial
              intelligence (such as ChatGPT, Harvey.AI, or Google Bard)
              or that any language drafted by generative artificial
              intelligence was checked for accuracy, using print
              reporters or traditional legal databases, by a human
              being."
